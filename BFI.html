British Film Institute Top Movies

    The dataset consists of the top 15 movies released in the UK each year from 2012 to 2021.
  • The British Film Institute (BFI) records weekend gross figures for every weekend.
  • To ensure consistency; the dataset for each year is taken from the weekend at the end of September/beginning of October.
